<p><p>The aqueous inkjet pigment dispersion market includes various types designed for different applications. Oil-based dispersions utilize oils to enhance pigment stability and provide excellent color intensity. Alkyd dispersions combine fatty acid-based resins with pigments for improved adhesion and durability, commonly used in industrial coatings. Epoxy dispersions offer superior chemical resistance and adhesion, making them ideal for specialty applications. Other dispersions may include acrylic or urethane-based formulations, each catering to specific performance and application needs in printing and coating industries.</p></p>
