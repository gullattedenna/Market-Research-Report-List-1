<p><strong>The Atherectomy Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Directional Atherectomy Devices</li><li>Orbital Atherectomy Devices</li><li>Rotational Atherectomy Devices</li><li>Laser Atherectomy Devices</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Atherectomy devices are specialized tools used to remove atherosclerotic plaque from blood vessels. Directional atherectomy devices cut and remove plaque in a specific direction, while orbital atherectomy devices use a rotating mechanism to ablate plaque circumferentially. Rotational atherectomy devices grind away plaque using a diamond-coated burr, making it effective in hard lesions. Laser atherectomy devices employ focused lasers to vaporize plaque, providing precision treatment. Each type serves distinct clinical needs in the management of cardiovascular disease.</p></p>
