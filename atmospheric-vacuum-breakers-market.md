<p><p>Atmospheric Vacuum Breakers (AVBs) are devices designed to prevent backflow in plumbing systems, ensuring that water supply is not contaminated by reverse siphoning. They function by breaking the vacuum created in a system when water flows away, allowing air to enter and maintain proper pressure. AVBs are widely used in irrigation, industrial, and commercial applications, providing a reliable and efficient solution for maintaining water quality and safeguarding against contamination.</p><p>The Atmospheric Vacuum Breakers Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 7% during the forecast period.
