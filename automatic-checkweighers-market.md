<p><strong>The Automatic Checkweighers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>In-Motion Checkweighers</li><li>Intermittent Checkweighers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automatic checkweighers market is primarily divided into two types: in-motion checkweighers and intermittent checkweighers. In-motion checkweighers are designed for continuous operation, allowing products to be weighed while moving through a production line, enhancing efficiency and speed in high-volume applications. Conversely, intermittent checkweighers operate in a stop-and-go manner, measuring product weight at specific intervals, which can be advantageous for quality control in slower-paced environments. Both types play crucial roles in maintaining product accuracy and compliance in various industries.</p></p>
