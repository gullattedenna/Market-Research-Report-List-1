<p><p>The bariatric toilet market primarily features two types: siphon jet and blowout jet models. Siphon jet toilets utilize a siphoning action to create a powerful flush, effectively removing waste with less water, making them suitable for heavier loads. In contrast, blowout jet toilets employ a high-pressure jet stream to forcefully clear waste, providing a more aggressive flushing action. Both designs cater to individuals with higher weight capacities, promoting ease of use and ensuring improved hygiene and comfort.</p></p>
