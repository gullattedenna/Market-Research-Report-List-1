<p><ul><li>Tissue Aortic Valve</li><li>Tissue Mitral Valve</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The bioprosthetic valve market comprises various types, notably tissue aortic valves and tissue mitral valves. Tissue aortic valves are designed to replace damaged aortic valves, using biological materials to ensure compatibility and reduce rejection rates. Conversely, tissue mitral valves replace malfunctioning mitral valves, aiding in effective blood flow regulation within the heart. Both valve types are popular due to their lower risk of thrombosis compared to mechanical valves, making them favorable choices for patients requiring valve replacement surgeries.</p></p>
