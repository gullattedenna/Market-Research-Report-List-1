<p><p>Cable and pipe locators are essential tools for detecting underground utilities. The market is divided into active and passive location types. Active location involves sending a signal through the cable or pipe, allowing for precise pinpointing with specialized receivers. In contrast, passive location relies on detecting existing signals from utilities, such as electromagnetic fields, without the need for additional signals. Each method serves distinct applications in construction, maintenance, and excavation, ensuring safety and efficiency in utility detection.</p></p>
