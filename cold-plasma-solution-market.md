<p><p>The cold plasma solution market encompasses two primary types: atmospheric cold plasma and low-pressure cold plasma. Atmospheric cold plasma operates at ambient pressure and is widely used for sterilization, surface modification, and wound healing due to its ease of integration into various applications. In contrast, low-pressure cold plasma functions in vacuum environments, making it ideal for specialized applications like semiconductor manufacturing and material processing. Both types leverage plasma's unique properties for diverse industrial and medical purposes, driving market growth.</p></p>
