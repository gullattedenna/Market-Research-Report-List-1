<p><p>The compostable plastics market comprises several key types. Polylactic Acid (PLA) is derived from renewable resources like cornstarch and is widely used in packaging and disposable items. Polyhydroxy Fatty Acid (PHA) is produced by microorganisms and is known for its biodegradability in marine environments. Thermoplastic Starch combines starch with other polymers for enhanced properties. Polybutylene Succinate (PBS) offers good flexibility and is used in various applications. Other materials include blends and innovative biopolymers catering to specific needs in sustainability.</p></p>
