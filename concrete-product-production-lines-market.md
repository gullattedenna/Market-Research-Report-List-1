<p><p>Concrete product production lines are categorized into fully automatic and semi-automatic systems based on the level of automation. Fully automatic lines operate with minimal human intervention, utilizing advanced robotics and automation technologies to enhance efficiency and consistency. They are ideal for high-volume production, reducing labor costs. In contrast, semi-automatic lines require some manual input while still automating key processes. This balance allows for greater flexibility and lower initial investment, catering to medium-sized operations that prioritize both efficiency and human oversight.</p></p>
