<p><p>The cooled infrared camera market comprises three main types based on the infrared spectrum: Cooled Long-wave Infrared (LWIR) cameras are optimized for thermal imaging, effective in detecting heat signatures, making them ideal for surveillance and industrial applications. Cooled Middle-wave Infrared (MWIR) cameras excel in various military and aerospace applications, offering high sensitivity and resolution for detecting subtle temperature differences. Cooled Short-wave Infrared (SWIR) cameras are used for applications like night vision and non-destructive testing, capturing information in the near-infrared spectrum.</p></p>
