<p><strong>The Cosmetics Laminate Tube Packaging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>ABL</li><li>PBL</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cosmetics laminate tube packaging primarily comes in two types: ABL (Aluminum Barrier Laminate) and PBL (Plastic Barrier Laminate). ABL tubes feature a barrier layer of aluminum, providing excellent protection against moisture, air, and light, making them suitable for products that require extended shelf life. In contrast, PBL tubes are made of layers of plastic materials, offering flexibility and a lightweight option, often used for products with less stringent protection needs. Both types ensure aesthetic appeal and functional integrity for cosmetic products.</p></p>
