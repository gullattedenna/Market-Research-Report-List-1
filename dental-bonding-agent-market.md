<p><strong>The Dental Bonding Agent Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Self-etch</li><li>Total-etch</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dental bonding agent market is primarily divided into two types: self-etch and total-etch bonding agents. Self-etch bonding agents integrate with the tooth structure by simultaneously etching and priming, simplifying the application process and reducing post-operative sensitivity. In contrast, total-etch bonding agents involve a multi-step process where the enamel and dentin are separately etched, allowing for stronger adhesion and enhanced durability. Each type caters to different dental needs and preferences, impacting clinical outcomes and patient satisfaction.</p></p>
