<p><strong>The Dental X ray Device Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Intraoral X-Ray Imaging</li><li>Extraoral X-Ray Imaging</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dental X-ray device market is divided into two main types: intraoral and extraoral imaging. Intraoral X-ray imaging involves placing the X-ray film or sensor inside the mouth, providing detailed images of individual teeth and surrounding structures, which aids in diagnosing cavities and other dental issues. Extraoral X-ray imaging, on the other hand, captures images from outside the mouth, often providing broader views of the jaw, teeth, and skull, useful for orthodontic assessments and treatment planning.</p></p>
