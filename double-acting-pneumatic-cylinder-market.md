<p><p>The double-acting pneumatic cylinder market includes various types such as rotary pneumatic cylinders and semi-rotary pneumatic cylinders. Rotary pneumatic cylinders convert air pressure into rotational motion, providing continuous turning for applications like conveyors or automated assembly lines. Semi-rotary pneumatic cylinders, on the other hand, offer limited rotation, typically used for clamping or positioning tasks in machinery. Both types enhance efficiency and precision in industrial processes, catering to diverse operational needs across manufacturing and automation sectors.</p></p>
