<p><strong>The E-paper Display (EPD) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electrophoretic Display (EPD)</li><li>Electrowetting Display (EWD)</li><li>Electrofluidic Display (EFD)</li><li>Interferometric Modulator Display (IMOD)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>E-paper displays (EPD) are innovative technologies designed for low-power, lightweight electronic displays. The Electrophoretic Display (EPD) utilizes charged particles in a fluid to create images, commonly seen in e-readers. Electrowetting Display (EWD) manipulates liquids on a surface for color variation. Electrofluidic Display (EFD) combines electronic fluid dynamics for enhanced images, while Interferometric Modulator Display (IMOD) uses microstructures to reflect light, offering vibrant colors. Each type caters to specific applications, emphasizing energy efficiency and visibility.</p></p>
