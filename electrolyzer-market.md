<p><ul><li>Traditional Alkaline Electroliser</li><li>PEM Electroliser</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electrolyzer market primarily consists of two main types: Traditional Alkaline Electrolyzers and Proton Exchange Membrane (PEM) Electrolyzers. Traditional Alkaline Electrolyzers utilize a liquid alkaline solution as an electrolyte, offering cost-effectiveness and durability, suitable for large-scale hydrogen production. In contrast, PEM Electrolyzers employ a solid polymer membrane, providing higher efficiency and rapid response to varying energy inputs, ideal for renewable energy integration. Each technology has its unique advantages, catering to distinct applications in the growing hydrogen economy.</p></p>
