<p><p>Endovascular Aneurysm Repair (EVAR) devices are minimally invasive surgical tools used to treat aneurysms, primarily abdominal aortic aneurysms (AAAs). These devices consist of a stent graft that is delivered through catheters into the vascular system, allowing for the reinforcement of the weakened arterial wall without the need for open surgery. The market for EVAR devices is witnessing significant growth driven by an increasing prevalence of vascular diseases, advancements in technology, and a growing elderly population.</p><p>Market trends indicate a shift towards more innovative and patient-friendly designs, such as fenestrated and branched stent grafts that enhance treatment options for complex aneurysms.
