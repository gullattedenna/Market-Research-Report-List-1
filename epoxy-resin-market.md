<p><p>The epoxy resin market is segmented into various types based on their chemical composition. DGBEA utilizes Bisphenol A and epichlorohydrin (ECH), while DGBEF combines Bisphenol F with ECH. Novolac resins are derived from formaldehyde and phenols, offering high thermal stability. Aliphatic epoxy resins are made from aliphatic alcohols, providing flexibility and UV resistance. Glycidylamine resins use aromatic amines and ECH for enhanced mechanical properties. Additionally, the "Others" category encompasses various alternative formulations catering to specific applications and performance needs.</p></p>
