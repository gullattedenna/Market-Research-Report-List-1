<p><p>Ethylene Propylene Rubber (EPR) is a synthetic rubber known for its excellent elasticity, weather resistance, and chemical stability. The market is primarily divided into two types: Ethylene Propylene Monomer (EPM) and Ethylene Propylene Diene Monomer (EPDM). EPM, a polymer with a saturated backbone, offers superior thermal and aging properties, while EPDM, which includes diene for cross-linking, is widely used in automotive, roofing, and electrical insulation applications due to its enhanced mechanical strength and ozone resistance.</p></p>
