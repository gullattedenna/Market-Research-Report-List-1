<p><strong>The Explosion Protection Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Explosion Venting System</li><li>Explosion Isolation System</li><li>Explosion Suppression</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The explosion protection market encompasses three primary types: explosion venting systems, explosion isolation systems, and explosion suppression systems. Explosion venting systems allow pressure relief during an explosion, directing the force away to minimize damage. Explosion isolation systems prevent flame propagation and pressure waves from traveling through ducts or piping. Explosion suppression systems utilize sensors and extinguishing agents to quickly detect and neutralize potential explosions, enhancing safety in industrial environments and protecting assets from explosive risks.</p></p>
