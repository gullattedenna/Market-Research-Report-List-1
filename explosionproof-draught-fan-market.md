<p><p>The explosionproof draught fan market consists of three main types: centrifugal, axial flow, and inclined flow fans. Centrifugal fans are designed to move air with high pressure through a system, making them ideal for applications requiring strong airflow resistance. Axial flow fans push air along the axis of the fan, providing efficient ventilation in larger spaces. Inclined flow fans combine features of both, offering flexibility in airflow direction and installation. Each type caters to specific industrial needs, ensuring safety in hazardous environments.</p></p>
