<p><p>The fiber ureteroscope market comprises three main types: flexible, semi-rigid, and rigid ureteroscopes. Flexible ureteroscopes are designed for intricate access and maneuverability within the urinary tract, ideal for complex procedures. Semi-rigid ureteroscopes offer a balance between flexibility and rigidity, providing stability while allowing some maneuvering. Rigid ureteroscopes are characterized by their inflexible nature, offering enhanced control and visibility during surgical interventions but are limited in access to certain anatomical areas. Each type serves specific clinical needs in urology.</p></p>
