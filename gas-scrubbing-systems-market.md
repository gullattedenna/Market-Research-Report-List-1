<p><p>Gas scrubbing systems are essential for removing pollutants from industrial emissions. Jet and venturi scrubbers utilize high-velocity gas jets to create a turbulent mixing of gas and liquid, effectively capturing contaminants. Orifice scrubbers employ a simple design where gas passes through small openings, promoting particle removal. Packed bed scrubbers consist of packing material that enhances contact between the gas and scrubbing liquid, providing efficient mass transfer and contaminant capture. Each type caters to different application needs and efficiency levels.</p></p>
