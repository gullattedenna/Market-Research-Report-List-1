<p><p>Geopolymer cement is an eco-friendly alternative to traditional Portland cement, produced using industrial byproducts or natural materials. **Slag-based geopolymer cement** utilizes blast furnace slag, offering high durability and chemical resistance. **Rock-based geopolymer cement** is derived from natural aluminosilicate rocks, providing sustainable and cost-effective solutions. **Fly ash-based geopolymer cement** uses fly ash, a coal combustion byproduct, reducing waste and carbon emissions. **Ferro-sialate-based geopolymer cement** incorporates iron-rich materials, enhancing strength and thermal stability. Each type caters to specific applications, promoting sustainability and reducing environmental impact.</p></p>
