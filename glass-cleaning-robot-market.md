<p><ul><li>Drag-wiper</li><li>Roller-wiper</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The glass cleaning robot market is primarily categorized into two types: drag-wiper and roller-wiper robots. Drag-wiper robots operate by sliding across the surface using a microfiber cloth or similar material, effectively removing dirt and grime. In contrast, roller-wiper robots employ rotating rollers that scrub the glass, offering enhanced cleaning efficiency and adaptability to different surface textures. Both types cater to various applications, including residential and commercial settings, addressing the rising demand for automated cleaning solutions.</p></p>
