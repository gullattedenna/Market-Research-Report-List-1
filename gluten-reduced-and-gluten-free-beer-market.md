<p><p>The gluten-reduced and gluten-free beer market includes two main types: dedicated gluten-free and gluten-reduced beers. Dedicated gluten-free beers are brewed from grains that naturally do not contain gluten, such as sorghum, rice, or buckwheat, making them safe for individuals with celiac disease or severe gluten sensitivities. In contrast, gluten-reduced beers are made from traditional grains like barley but undergo an enzyme treatment to lower gluten levels. While they may be suitable for some gluten-intolerant consumers, they are not safe for those with celiac disease.</p></p>
