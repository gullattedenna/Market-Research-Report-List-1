<p><p>The grain sorting line market is primarily categorized into two types: optical sorting machines and gravity sorting machines. Optical sorting machines utilize advanced imaging technology to identify and separate grains based on size, shape, and color, enhancing the purity and quality of the output. On the other hand, gravity sorting machines rely on specific gravity differences to segregate grains, effectively removing lighter or heavier impurities. Both technologies are essential for optimizing grain processing efficiency and ensuring high-quality agricultural products.</p></p>
