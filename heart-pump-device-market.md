<p><ul><li>Ventricular Assist Devices (VADs)</li><li>Intra-aortic Balloon Pumps (IABPs)</li><li>Total Artificial Heart (TAH)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The heart pump device market encompasses several types of technologies designed to support or replace heart function. Ventricular Assist Devices (VADs) are mechanical pumps that assist the heart's ventricles, aiding in blood circulation for patients with severe heart failure. Intra-aortic Balloon Pumps (IABPs) enhance coronary blood flow and reduce workload by inflating and deflating a balloon in the aorta. Total Artificial Hearts (TAHs) replace the entire heart function, providing a complete mechanical solution for patients awaiting transplants or those ineligible for surgery.</p></p>
