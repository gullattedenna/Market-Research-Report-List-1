<p><p>The heating mats market includes three main types: microwavable heating pads, electric heating pads, and chemical heating pads. Microwavable heating pads are typically filled with materials like rice or gel and provide localized heat after being heated in a microwave. Electric heating pads use electricity to generate consistent warmth, often with adjustable settings for temperature control. Chemical heating pads generate heat through exothermic chemical reactions, offering convenience as disposable options for temporary relief without the need for power sources.</p></p>
