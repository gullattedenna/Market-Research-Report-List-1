<p><p>The Heavy Duty Axle Shaft market includes components like Propeller Shafts and Half Shafts, essential for power transmission in vehicles. Propeller Shafts, also known as drive shafts, transfer torque from the engine to the wheels, commonly used in trucks and heavy-duty vehicles. Half Shafts, or axle shafts, connect the differential to the wheels, enabling rotational motion. Both are critical for durability and performance in demanding applications, catering to industries like automotive, construction, and agriculture, where robust load-bearing and torque-handling capabilities are required.</p></p>
