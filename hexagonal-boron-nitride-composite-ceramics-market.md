<p><p>The Hexagonal Boron Nitride Composite Ceramics market is categorized into two main types based on their structural characteristics: Graphite Structure and Diamond Structure Hexagonal Boron Nitride Composite Ceramics. Graphite Structure composites feature a layered, planar arrangement, providing excellent thermal conductivity and lubrication properties, making them suitable for high-temperature applications. In contrast, Diamond Structure composites possess a three-dimensional arrangement, offering enhanced hardness and wear resistance, ideal for cutting tools and abrasive applications, thus catering to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
