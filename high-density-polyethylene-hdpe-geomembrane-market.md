<p><p>The High Density Polyethylene (HDPE) geomembrane market comprises three primary types: single layer, double layer, and three layer geomembranes. Single layer geomembranes consist of a single sheet of HDPE, offering a cost-effective solution for various applications. Double layer geomembranes incorporate two sheets, enhancing durability and resistance to punctures, making them suitable for more demanding environments. Three layer geomembranes provide additional reinforcement and flexibility, often used in specialized applications requiring superior performance and long-term reliability in containment systems.</p></p>
