<p><p>The hot air tunnel market primarily consists of two types: single air duct and multiple air duct systems. Single air duct systems utilize one channel to distribute heated air, making them simpler and often more cost-effective for smaller spaces. In contrast, multiple air duct systems feature several ducts, allowing for more uniform heat distribution across larger areas and increased control over temperature settings. Both types cater to various industrial and commercial applications, enhancing efficiency in processes requiring regulated heating environments.</p></p>
