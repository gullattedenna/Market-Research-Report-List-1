<p><ul><li>Non-Reactive Hot Melt Adhesives</li><li>Reactive Hot Melt Adhesives</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Hot Melt Polyurethane Adhesive market is divided into two main types: Non-Reactive Hot Melt Adhesives and Reactive Hot Melt Adhesives. Non-Reactive Hot Melt Adhesives are thermoplastic materials that provide immediate adhesion upon cooling, offering ease of application and versatility for various substrates. In contrast, Reactive Hot Melt Adhesives undergo a chemical reaction during curing, resulting in stronger bonds and higher resistance to environmental factors. Both types are widely used in industries such as woodworking, automotive, and packaging for their reliability and performance.</p></p>
