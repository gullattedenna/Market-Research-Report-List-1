<p><p>The laser communication market is segmented into three main types: lasers, modulators, and others. Lasers serve as the primary light source for communication, enabling high-speed data transmission over long distances. Modulators adjust the laser light's properties, encoding data for efficient transmission and improving signal integrity. The "others" category includes ancillary components such as detectors, fiber optics, and amplifiers that support laser communication systems. Together, these elements drive advancements in telecommunications, space exploration, and secure data transfer solutions.</p></p>
