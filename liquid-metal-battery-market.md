<p><p>The liquid metal battery market comprises three primary types: Mg-Sb, Pb-Sb, and Na-S batteries. Mg-Sb batteries utilize magnesium and antimony, offering high energy density and safety. Pb-Sb batteries combine lead and antimony, providing advantages in scalability and cost-effectiveness for grid storage. Na-S batteries leverage sodium and sulfur, known for high efficiency and durability, making them ideal for large-scale energy applications. Each type serves distinct energy storage needs, contributing to advancements in renewable energy integration and grid stability.</p></p>
