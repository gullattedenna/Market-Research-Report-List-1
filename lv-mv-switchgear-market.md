<p><p>The LV and MV switchgear market comprises three main types: Air Insulated Switchgear (AIS), Gas Insulated Switchgear (GIS), and others. AIS uses air as the insulating medium, making it suitable for open spaces and less expensive but larger in size. GIS employs gas, often SF6, for insulation, allowing for compact design and reduced footprint, ideal for urban environments. Other types include hybrid systems and modular switchgears, offering varied applications based on specific installation needs and environmental conditions.</p></p>
