Rubber-based adhesives deliver quick stickiness and conformability, often used in general-purpose applications. Non-adhesive tapes, while lacking adhesive properties, serve specialized functions like bundling or protective layering, catering to diverse industrial and consumer needs across sectors.</p></p>
