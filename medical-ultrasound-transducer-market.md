<p><p>The medical ultrasound transducer market includes several types, each tailored for specific applications. Linear transducers provide high-resolution images for vascular and musculoskeletal assessments. Convex transducers offer a broader field of view, making them ideal for abdominal and obstetric imaging. Phased array transducers are compact and can produce images of the heart, useful in cardiology. Endocavitary transducers are designed for internal examinations, such as transvaginal or transrectal ultrasound. Other types encompass specialty transducers for diverse diagnostic needs.</p></p>
