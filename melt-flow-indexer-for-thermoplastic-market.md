<p><p>A Melt Flow Indexer measures the flow characteristics of thermoplastics, crucial for market applications. Melt Mass Flow Rate (MFR) tests assess the mass of material flowing through a specific die under set conditions, indicating viscosity and processability. Melt Volume Flow Rate (MVR) tests determine the volume of material flowing in a given time, providing insights into thermal stability and flow behavior. Both tests are essential for quality control, ensuring materials meet industry standards and performance requirements for diverse applications.</p></p>
