<p><p>MEMS accelerometers are small, lightweight devices used in consumer electronics to measure acceleration and motion. The market is segmented by axis: 1-axis accelerometers measure movement along one direction, while 2-axis accelerometers capture motion in two dimensions. 3-axis accelerometers track movement in three dimensions, commonly found in smartphones and wearables. 6-axis accelerometers combine 3-axis acceleration with gyroscope data for orientation tracking. 9-axis accelerometers integrate 3-axis accelerometers, gyroscopes, and magnetometers, providing comprehensive motion sensing for advanced applications like virtual reality.</p></p>
