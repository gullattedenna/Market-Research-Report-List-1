<p><ul><li>Oil Soluble Passivator</li><li>Water Soluble Passivator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Metal Deactivators market encompasses two primary types: oil-soluble passivators and water-soluble passivators. Oil-soluble passivators are designed for use in hydrocarbon-based systems, effectively inhibiting metal-catalyzed degradation in fuels and lubricants. In contrast, water-soluble passivators are utilized in aqueous systems, providing protection against corrosion and oxidation in water-based formulations. Both types are essential in enhancing the stability and longevity of various products across industries, including automotive, coatings, and plastics, by mitigating the adverse effects of metal contaminants.</p></p>
