<p><p>The modified linseed oils market features three main types: stand oil, boiled linseed oil, and raw linseed oil. Stand oil is a thicker, slow-drying oil used in paints and varnishes, providing a smooth finish. Boiled linseed oil is heat-treated, enhancing its drying time and making it ideal for wood finishing and as a paint binder. Raw linseed oil is minimally processed, offering a slower drying time and is often used in artistic applications and traditional oil paints.</p></p>
