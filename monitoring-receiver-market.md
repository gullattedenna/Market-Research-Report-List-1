<p><p>Monitoring receivers can be categorized into two main market types: automatic and semi-automatic. Automatic monitoring receivers operate independently, continuously analyzing signals and generating alerts without human intervention. They ensure efficient and consistent surveillance of frequencies. In contrast, semi-automatic monitoring receivers require some level of human input for operation, allowing users to manually adjust settings or respond to alerts while still providing automated functionalities. Both types play crucial roles in managing radio frequency activities and ensuring compliance with regulations.</p></p>
