<p><p>The Nanoparticle Analysis Market encompasses several techniques for characterizing nanoparticle properties. Dynamic Light Scattering (DLS) measures particle size and distribution through light scattering in a suspension. Nanoparticle Tracking Analysis (NTA) visualizes individual particles, providing size and concentration data. Laser Diffraction Method (LDM) utilizes laser light scattering to determine particle size distributions, effective for larger ranges. Other methods may include electron microscopy and atomic force microscopy, offering additional insights into nanoparticle morphology and surface properties, catering to diverse applications in research and industry.</p></p>
