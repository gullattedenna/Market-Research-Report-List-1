<p><p>The oilfield exploration market comprises various methods to locate and assess oil reserves. The geological method involves analyzing rock formations and surface characteristics to identify potential sites. The geophysical method utilizes seismic surveys and subsurface imaging to detect oil deposits beneath the surface. The geochemical method assesses soil and water samples for hydrocarbon indicators. Lastly, the drilling method involves exploratory drilling to confirm the presence of oil and gather data on reservoir characteristics, facilitating informed extraction decisions.</p></p>
