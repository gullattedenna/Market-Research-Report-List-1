<p><p>The orthopedic phototherapy lamp market is primarily divided into two types: infrared and visible light. Infrared lamps emit wavelengths that penetrate deeper tissues, providing relief from pain, inflammation, and muscle stiffness, making them ideal for deeper muscle and joint issues. Visible light lamps, on the other hand, cater to surface-level treatments, promoting healing through enhanced cellular activity and improved blood circulation. Both types serve distinct therapeutic purposes, addressing various orthopedic conditions and contributing to rehabilitation and recovery processes.</p></p>
