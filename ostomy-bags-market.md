<p><p>The ostomy bags market comprises two main types: one-piece and two-piece bags. One-piece bags integrate both the pouch and the skin barrier into a single unit, offering simplicity and ease of use, making them ideal for active individuals. In contrast, two-piece bags consist of a separate skin barrier and pouch, allowing for flexibility in changing the pouch without removing the barrier. This distinction caters to varying user preferences and lifestyles, influencing their market demand and growth.</p></p>
