<p><p>Particulate reinforced composites are categorized into two main types: large particle composites and dispersion strengthened composites. Large particle composites incorporate larger particles that enhance mechanical properties and stiffness, making them suitable for applications requiring greater strength. In contrast, dispersion strengthened composites utilize smaller particles uniformly distributed within the matrix to improve thermal stability, wear resistance, and strength. Both types are widely used in industries like automotive, aerospace, and construction, offering enhanced performance characteristics compared to traditional materials.</p></p>
