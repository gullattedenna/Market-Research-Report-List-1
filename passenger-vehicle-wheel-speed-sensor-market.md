<p><strong>The Passenger Vehicle Wheel Speed Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hall Type</li><li>Magnetic Electric Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The passenger vehicle wheel speed sensor market comprises two primary types: Hall effect sensors and magnetic electric sensors. Hall effect sensors utilize a magnetic field to detect wheel rotation, providing accurate speed readings essential for vehicle stability control and anti-lock braking systems. In contrast, magnetic electric sensors rely on variable magnetic fields generated by rotating gears or wheel teeth, producing electrical signals proportional to speed. Both types are crucial for enhancing vehicle safety and performance, serving diverse automotive applications.</p></p>
