<p><ul><li>Polybutene-1 Homopolymer (PB-H)</li><li>Polybutene-1 Random Copolymer (PB-R)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PB pipes market comprises two main types: Polybutene-1 Homopolymer (PB-H) and Polybutene-1 Random Copolymer (PB-R). PB-H is characterized by high strength and good chemical resistance, making it ideal for pressure piping applications, while PB-R offers flexibility and enhanced thermal properties, suitable for use in plumbing and heating systems. Both types leverage polybutene-1's low thermal conductivity and corrosion resistance, catering to various industrial and residential applications in plumbing, heating, and gas distribution systems.</p></p>
