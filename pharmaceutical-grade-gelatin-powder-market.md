<p><p>The pharmaceutical grade gelatin powder market is primarily segmented into two types: bone and skin gelatin. Skin gelatin, sourced from animal hides, is more versatile and commonly found in dietary supplements and food applications. Both types are valued for their gelling properties and are crucial in drug formulation, offering compatibility with various pharmaceutical products and enhancing bioavailability.</p></p>
