<p><p>The Polyester Composite Geogrid market is segmented into two main types: Biaxial Tension and Uniaxial Tension. Biaxial Tension geogrids are designed to provide reinforcement in both horizontal and vertical directions, making them suitable for applications such as soil stabilization and retaining walls. In contrast, Uniaxial Tension geogrids are primarily engineered to resist forces in a single direction, ideal for use in applications like base reinforcement under roads and pavements. Both types enhance structural integrity and longevity in geotechnical applications.</p></p>
