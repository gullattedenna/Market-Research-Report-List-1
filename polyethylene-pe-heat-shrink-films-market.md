<p><p>The Polyethylene (PE) heat shrink films market is primarily categorized into two types: LDPE (Low-Density Polyethylene) and LLDPE (Linear Low-Density Polyethylene) heat shrink films. LDPE heat shrink films are known for their flexibility and ease of processing, making them ideal for packaging food and consumer products. In contrast, LLDPE heat shrink films offer enhanced strength, puncture resistance, and better sealing properties, making them suitable for industrial applications and durable packaging solutions. Both types cater to diverse market needs.</p></p>
