<p><p>The Polymer Dispersed Liquid Crystal (PDLC) film market is segmented into two main types: None-Adhesive PDLC film and Self-Adhesive PDLC film. None-Adhesive PDLC film is typically applied using mounting techniques, allowing for flexibility in installation on various surfaces. In contrast, Self-Adhesive PDLC film comes with a peel-and-stick backing, making it easier to apply directly onto glass or other substrates without additional adhesive materials. Both types are utilized for applications such as privacy screens, smart windows, and decorative elements.</p></p>
