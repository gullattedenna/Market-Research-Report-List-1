<p><p>The Polytetrafluoroethylene (PTFE) coatings market primarily consists of two types: powder coating and liquid coating. Powder coating involves applying a dry, powdered form of PTFE that is then cured to form a durable finish, offering excellent resistance to chemicals and high temperatures. Liquid coating, on the other hand, consists of a PTFE suspension that is applied through spraying or dipping, providing a smooth and uniform layer. Both types enhance surface properties like non-stickiness, corrosion resistance, and reduced friction across various applications.</p></p>
