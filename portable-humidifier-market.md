<p><strong>The Portable Humidifier Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Warm Mist Humidifiers</li><li>Cool Mist Humidifiers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The portable humidifier market offers two primary types: warm mist and cool mist humidifiers. Warm mist humidifiers heat water to produce steam, which is then cooled slightly before being released into the air, making them ideal for relieving congestion and enhancing comfort in colder climates. In contrast, cool mist humidifiers disperse room-temperature water vapor, promoting humidity without increasing the room temperature. They are particularly beneficial in warmer environments, helping to alleviate dryness without the risk of burns.</p></p>
