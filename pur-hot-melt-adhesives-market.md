<p><p>The PUR Hot-Melt Adhesives market is categorized into two main types: Non-Reactive and Reactive PUR Hot-Melt Adhesives. Non-Reactive PUR adhesives provide immediate bonding and are used for applications requiring quick set times. They typically offer good strength but may have limitations in heat and moisture resistance. In contrast, Reactive PUR Hot-Melt Adhesives undergo a chemical curing process after application, resulting in enhanced durability, moisture resistance, and adhesion properties, making them ideal for demanding applications in various industries.</p></p>
