<p><p>Roach traps are categorized into three main types: bait stations, granular baits, and gel baits. Bait stations contain attractants and insecticides, allowing roaches to enter and consume the poison without chemical exposure to pets or humans. Granular baits consist of small pellets that can be spread in areas where roaches are found, offering long-lasting control. Gel baits are formulated as viscous substances that can be applied discreetly, enticing roaches to eat the bait and eliminate them effectively.</p></p>
