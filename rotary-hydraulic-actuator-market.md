<p><strong>The Rotary Hydraulic Actuator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rack and Pinion</li><li>Vane Type</li><li>Combination type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rotary hydraulic actuator market comprises three main types: rack and pinion, vane type, and combination type. Rack and pinion actuators convert hydraulic pressure into rotational motion using a gear system, ideal for precise positioning. Vane type actuators utilize rotating vanes for smoother and continuous motion, suited for applications requiring rotational torque. Combination type actuators merge features of both systems, offering versatility for diverse industrial uses. Each type serves specific operational needs across various sectors, including manufacturing and automation.</p></p>
