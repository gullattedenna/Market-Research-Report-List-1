<p><p>Roundness and cylindrical profile measuring instruments are essential for assessing the geometric accuracy of cylindrical parts. The market is primarily categorized into sensor rotary and worktable rotary types. Sensor rotary instruments employ precise sensors that capture roundness data as the part rotates, ensuring high accuracy in measurements. Worktable rotary instruments, on the other hand, fix the sensor while the test piece rotates, allowing for detailed analysis of cylindrical profiles. Both types serve critical roles in quality control across various manufacturing sectors.</p></p>
