<p><p>The router market consists of three primary types: Regular Routers, Core Routers, and Edge Routers. Regular Routers are typically used in homes and small businesses, managing basic data traffic. Core Routers operate within the backbone of networks, handling large volumes of data at high speeds between various networks. Edge Routers connect different networks to the core and manage data exchange with external networks, ensuring efficient traffic routing. Each type serves distinct roles in optimizing network performance and connectivity.</p></p>
