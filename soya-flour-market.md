<p><p>The soya flour market comprises three main types: natural or full-fat soya flour, low-fat soya flour, and defatted soya flour. Natural or full-fat soya flour retains all the oil, providing rich flavor and higher fat content. Low-fat soya flour has reduced fat, making it a healthier option while still offering protein and fiber. Defatted soya flour, however, has most of the oil removed, resulting in a product high in protein and low in fat, suitable for various health-conscious applications.</p></p>
