<p><p>The spectroscopy instruments market encompasses various techniques tailored for material analysis. Raman spectroscopy measures molecular vibrations, providing insights into chemical composition. Fluorescence spectroscopy detects emitted light from samples, useful for biological and chemical analysis. Near infrared spectroscopy evaluates overtones and combinations of molecular vibrations, commonly used in agriculture and pharmaceuticals. Fourier Transform Infrared Spectroscopy (FTIR) analyzes infrared light absorption, revealing functional groups in compounds. The “Others” category includes techniques like UV-Vis spectroscopy and mass spectrometry, expanding the market's diversity.</p></p>
