<p><p>The stainless crowns market is divided into two main types: temporary and long-term. They provide short-term protection until a more permanent restoration is placed. In contrast, long-term stainless crowns are designed for sustained use, often exceeding one month, and are employed for more durable dental restorations, offering enhanced strength and longevity for patients with extensive dental needs.</p></p>
