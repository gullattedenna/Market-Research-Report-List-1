<p><ul><li>Jet Condenser</li><li>Surface Condenser</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The steam condenser market comprises two primary types: jet condensers and surface condensers. Jet condensers utilize a direct contact method where steam mixes with cooling water, leading to efficient heat transfer but requiring a larger water supply. Surface condensers, on the other hand, employ a heat exchanger to separate steam and cooling water, enhancing efficiency and minimizing water usage. Both types are essential in power plants and industrial applications, catering to different operational requirements and environmental considerations.</p></p>
