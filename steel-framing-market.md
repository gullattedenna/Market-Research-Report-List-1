The **Modular Structural Frame** is prefabricated, cost-effective, and versatile, suitable for quick assembly in commercial and industrial projects. The **Single Slope Frame Style** features a sloped roof for drainage, commonly used in smaller buildings like garages or sheds, offering simplicity and affordability. Each type caters to specific needs, balancing functionality, cost, and design flexibility in steel framing applications.</p></p>
