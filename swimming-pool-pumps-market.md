<p><p>The swimming pool pump market consists of three main types: Single Speed, Two Speed, and Variable Speed pumps. Two Speed pumps offer a low-speed setting for regular filtration and a high-speed option for tasks like vacuuming, balancing efficiency and power. Variable Speed pumps are the most advanced, allowing users to adjust speeds for optimal energy savings and tailored performance, making them increasingly popular for eco-conscious pool owners.</p></p>
