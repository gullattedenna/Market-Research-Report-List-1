<p><strong>The TCPP Flame Retardant Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Endothermic Degradation</li><li>Dilution of Gas Phase</li><li>Gas Phase Radical Quenching</li><li>Thermal Shielding</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The TCPP flame retardant market encompasses various mechanisms. Endothermic degradation absorbs heat during combustion, lowering temperature. Dilution of gas phase reduces flammable gases, minimizing ignition potential. Gas phase radical quenching interrupts combustion processes by neutralizing free radicals. Finally, thermal shielding forms a protective barrier that slows heat transfer, enhancing material stability. Together, these mechanisms enhance fire safety in various applications, making TCPP a valuable choice for manufacturers seeking effective flame retardant solutions.</p></p>
