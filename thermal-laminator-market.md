<p><strong>The Thermal Laminator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Coating Thermal Laminator</li><li>Pre-coated Thermal Laminator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The thermal laminator market consists of two main types: coating thermal laminators and pre-coated thermal laminators. Coating thermal laminators apply a liquid adhesive layer to substrates before laminating, offering flexibility in finishes and thickness. Conversely, pre-coated thermal laminators utilize films that are already coated with adhesive, streamlining the process by eliminating the need for additional coating, making it quicker and easier to use. Both types enhance durability and visual appeal of printed materials, catering to different user preferences.</p></p>
