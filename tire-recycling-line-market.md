Rubber blocks are produced by compressing recycled tire materials into solid forms, suitable for various applications. Colloidal rubber involves fine processing to create a viscous liquid, ideal for coatings or adhesives. Rubber powder is generated through grinding used tires into fine particles, widely utilized in numerous industries, including construction and automotive, enhancing product durability and sustainability. These segments reflect the diverse applications of recycled tire materials.</p></p>
