<p><p>The **Vehicle Camshaft Market** includes three main types: **Cast Camshafts**, **Assembled Camshafts**, and **Forged Camshafts**. Cast camshafts are made by pouring molten metal into molds, offering cost-effectiveness and suitability for standard applications. Assembled camshafts are built by joining separate components, allowing flexibility and customization for specific engine designs. Forged camshafts are created by shaping metal under high pressure, resulting in superior strength and durability, ideal for high-performance engines. Each type caters to different automotive needs, balancing cost, performance, and manufacturing complexity.</p></p>
