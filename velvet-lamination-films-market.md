<p><ul><li>Thermal Velvet Film</li><li>Wet Velvet Film</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Velvet Lamination Films market is primarily categorized into Thermal Velvet Film and Wet Velvet Film. Thermal Velvet Film utilizes heat to bond the film to substrates, providing a smooth, luxurious texture ideal for premium packaging and branding. In contrast, Wet Velvet Film employs adhesives applied to surfaces before lamination, allowing for more flexibility in application and design. Both types enhance aesthetics while offering protective features, making them popular choices in various industries, including cosmetics, fashion, and consumer goods.</p></p>
