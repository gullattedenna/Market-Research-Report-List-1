<p><ul><li>Spring Type</li><li>Hydraulic Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vertical mill market is categorized into two primary types: spring type and hydraulic type. Spring type vertical mills utilize mechanical springs to provide the necessary tension and pressure during milling operations, offering simplicity and lower initial costs. In contrast, hydraulic type vertical mills employ hydraulic systems to exert force, enabling precise adjustments and better control over milling parameters. Both types cater to different industries, with spring types typically favored for smaller operations and hydraulic types preferred for larger, more complex milling tasks.</p></p>
