<p><p>The Waste to Diesel market converts waste into diesel fuel through technologies like gasification, pyrolysis, depolymerization, and incineration. Gasification transforms waste into syngas, which is then refined into diesel. Pyrolysis thermally decomposes waste in the absence of oxygen, producing bio-oil for diesel conversion. Depolymerization breaks down complex waste polymers into simpler hydrocarbons for fuel. Incineration burns waste to generate heat, which can power processes to produce synthetic diesel. These methods address waste management while creating renewable energy, reducing reliance on fossil fuels and minimizing environmental impact.</p></p>
