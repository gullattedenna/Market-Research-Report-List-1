<p><p>Water-cooled heat recovery Variable Refrigerant Flow (VRF) systems are designed for efficient heating and cooling in buildings. In 2-pipe systems, a single refrigerant line alternates between heating and cooling modes, making it simpler but limiting simultaneous temperature control. In contrast, 3-pipe systems utilize two refrigerant lines for simultaneous heating and cooling, allowing different zones to operate independently. This flexibility enhances comfort and energy efficiency, catering to diverse applications in commercial and residential settings, contributing to the growing market demand.</p></p>
